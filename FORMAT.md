## ISO Conventions
### Calendar date
1) Complete Representations
  - Basic: __YYYYMMDD__
  - Extended: __YYYY-MM-DD__
2) Reduced Accuracy
  - Specific Month
    - Basic: __YYYY-MM__
  - Specific Year
    - Basic: __YYYY__
  - Specific Century
    - Basic: __YY__

### Ordinal Date
1) Complete Representations
  - Basic: __YYYYDDD__
  - Extended: __YYYY-DD__

### Week Date
1) Complete Representation
  - Basic: __YYYYWwwD__
  - Extended: __YYYY-Www-D__
2) Reduced Accuracy
  - Basic: __YYYYWww__
  - Extended: __YYYY-Www__
